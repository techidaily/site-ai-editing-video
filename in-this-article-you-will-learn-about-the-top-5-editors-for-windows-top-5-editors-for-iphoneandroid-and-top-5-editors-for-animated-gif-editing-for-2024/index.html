Steps to Blur Background in Adobe After Effects

Adobe After Effects is an amazing tool for applying aesthetic visual effects to your videos. To make background blur in After Effects easily, check the following steps:

Step1 Open Adobe After Effects and upload the video clip you want to edit. Once done, double-click the video you have added to the timeline to select the clip you want to blur.

Step2 Now head to the ROTO brush displayed on top and click on it. The green circle can be used to select the part you would need. Step3 Once you have made the selection, play the video to do the calculations. After giving some time to do calculations, copy the layer from the timeline. Step4 Now adjust the feather value from the left side to 20% on Layer 1. Once done, you can see the results in the preview window.
