**Step 2**: Go to the **Edit & Create** option on the right corner on the top, and then select **Add slo-mo** option.

**Step 3:** Using the slider, you can determine how slowly you want it to go. Microsoft has built-in support for "**Slow**" and "**Super-Slow**" performance levels. Drag the slide to the right end will trigger the super slow mode.

![windows 10 photos adjust slow motion speed](https://images.wondershare.com/filmora/article-images/windows-10-photos-adjust-slow-motion-speed.jpg)

**Step 4:** Specifiy the part that you want to apply slow motion in the playback bar at the bottom, and then save the video that you have just [created with slow motion effects on Windows 10](https://tools.techidaily.com/wondershare/filmora/download/).
