● What are the essential elements of video editing?

Video editing comprises of 7 fundamental elements, namely, rough cut, picture lock, sound mix, visual effects, lower thirds, color correction and title and end cards. Following the aforesaid sequence for editing videos is necessary for achieving affordable results.

● What is meant by the 321 rule of video editing?

The 321 rule is universally applicable to all forms of video edits. The rule asks you to maintain a set of 3 copies for each one of your creations at 2 separate locations. Adhering to the rule ensures safe keep and quick access of the edited footages in times of need.
